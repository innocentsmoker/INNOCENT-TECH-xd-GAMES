NEON RUSH

A Cyberpunk Street Runner Experience

The World

In the sprawling, rain-slicked metropolis of Neo-Kyoto, you are Kaito, a disillusioned corporate drone who discovers a secret: the city is a lie. A powerful corporation, OmniGen, has layered a pervasive augmented reality network over the entire urban landscape, projecting a world of pristine order and consumer bliss to pacify the populace.

By hacking a prototype device onto your neural interface, you can see through this "Mirage" to the gritty, decaying, and dangerously real city beneath.

The Mission

The game is a high-octane, third-person parkour adventure where your primary goal is to survive, expose the truth, and outrun the corporate enforcers known as "Sanitizers" who see you as a glitch in their system.

The core gameplay loop is a desperate, fluid dash through the city's vertical and horizontal spaces, where the environment itself is a shifting, deceptive opponent.

Dual Reality System

Switch between the pristine corporate "Mirage" and the gritty reality beneath. Use both views to navigate the city, with paths and obstacles changing between realities.

Fluid Parkour Mechanics

Experience seamless movement with wall-running, precision jumps, zip-line transfers, and slide maneuvers. Chain moves together to maintain your "Flow" state for bonus points.

Dynamic Cityscape

Neo-Kyoto is a living, breathing city with day-night cycles, changing weather, and evolving districts that react to your actions and notoriety level.

Gameplay Mechanics

Movement is the soul of Neon Rush. It is designed to be fluid, intuitive, and immensely satisfying. Kaito's parkour is not just about running and jumping; it's an art form. You will fluidly chain together wall runs, precision jumps, zip-line transfers using grappling hooks on exposed beams, and slide under obstacles with a sense of weight and momentum that makes you feel like a force of nature.

Your hacking device allows for limited environmental manipulation, such as briefly disabling a flickering neon sign to create a dark hiding spot or overloading a power conduit to create a temporary electromagnetic pulse that stalls pursuing drones.

Progression & Upgrade System

Your progression is tied directly to your notoriety and your ability to manipulate the city's digital and physical layers. As you perform daring runs, evade Sanitizers, and shatter OmniGen's data-nodes to "leak" the truth to the underground net, your "Exposure Meter" fills.

Hacking Device

Upgrade your neural interface to extend Mirage vision duration, enhance pulse abilities, or add new environmental manipulation modules.

Parkour Skills

Unlock advanced movement techniques like Rebound Jumps and Pulse Slides that allow you to access previously unreachable areas.

Stealth Enhancements

Improve your ability to evade detection with camouflage tech, sound-dampening footwear, and decoy projection systems.
